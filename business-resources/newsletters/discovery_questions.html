IT Support & Maintenance Discovery

  • Do you have someone on staff who handles all your IT issues, or do you call someone when things break?
  • How quickly can you get help when your computers or network stop working?
  • What's the longest you've been without email or internet access?
  • Do you ever feel like you're always fixing the same problems over and over?
  • How often do your employees have to restart their computers or deal with slow performance?
  • How much time does your team spend each week dealing with technology problems instead of focusing on your business?

Security & Data Protection Discovery

  • How do you back up your important business files?
  • What would happen if you lost all your data tomorrow?
  • Have you ever worried about cyber security or had any close calls?
  • Do you feel confident that your business data is properly protected?
  • How do you handle software updates and security patches?

Business Impact Assessment

  • If I told you there was a way to reduce your IT problems by 80%, what would that be worth to your business?
  • How much does it cost when your team can't work because of technology issues?
  • What could your team accomplish if they never had to worry about computer problems?
  • Have you ever calculated how much time your staff spends dealing with IT issues instead of serving customers?
  • On a scale of 1-10, how satisfied are you with your current IT situation?

Decision-Making Process Discovery

  • Who typically makes decisions about technology purchases for your company?
  • How do you usually handle major technology decisions or upgrades?
  • What's your process when you need to solve a big technology problem?
  • Do you prefer to fix things as they break, or would you rather prevent problems before they happen?

Current Situation Assessment

  • What's working well with your current technology setup?
  • If you could wave a magic wand and fix one technology problem, what would it be?
  • How often do technology issues interrupt your normal business operations?
  • What's the most frustrating technology problem you deal with regularly?

Qualification & Next Steps Discovery

  • How open would you be to having someone take a look at your current setup and identify potential improvements?
  • Would it be worth 30 minutes to learn how to prevent most of your current IT problems?
  • If I could connect you with someone who specializes in solving exactly these types of issues for businesses like yours, would that be helpful?
  • Would you like to see what a proper business technology setup could look like for your company?

When to Use Discovery Questions

Best For:
  • Customer has shown interest in solving their IT problems
  • You need to quantify business impact
  • Building business case / Qualifying for MSP services
  • Understanding decision-making process
Key Principles:
  • Ask follow-up questions based on their specific responses
  • Focus on business impact, not technical features
  • Use their own words when reflecting back problems
  • Position as consultative partner, not salesperson
