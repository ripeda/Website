# In-House Apple Repairs

<img src="{{ site.baseurl | prepend: site.url }}/images/repairs/repair.png" alt="In-house Apple Repairs" style="width: 100%;">

As an Apple Authorized Repair Provider, we offer a full range of repair services for your Apple device fleet. We're trained using the same rigorous standards that Apple requires of its own technicians and use the same genuine Apple parts to deliver Apple-certified repairs.

And if the device is covered under AppleCare, the repair savings are passed on to you!

All repairs come with a complimentary loaner device to keep you productive while we work on your machine.
